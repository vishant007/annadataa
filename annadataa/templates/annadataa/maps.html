Explore Crops by Indian States

Welcome to our interactive map that allows you to explore the agricultural diversity of India. Hover over different states to discover the crops that flourish in each region. This information can be a valuable resource for farmers, researchers, and anyone interested in the agricultural landscape of India.

How to Use:

  • Simply hover your cursor over an Indian state on the map.
  • Information about the primary crops cultivated in that state will appear in a pop-up box.

Why is this Information Valuable?

Understanding the crops grown in different Indian states is essential for various reasons: -Agricultural Practices: Farmers can benefit from knowledge of the predominant crops in their region and explore potential crops for diversification.Consumer Awareness:Consumers can gain insights into the source of their food and the variety of crops produced across India.
